How to Play

Objective

Your goal is to outscore the CPU by the end of the 4th quarter. Make strategic plays, avoid penalties, and drive down the field to score!

Game Flow

  • Each quarter lasts 10 minutes (game time).
  • You can choose from four plays: Run, Pass, Field Goal, or Razzle Dazzle.
  • Yardage gained or lost depends on your play type and RNG (random number generator).

Play Options

  • Run: Gain consistent yardage with lower risk. Beware of holding penalties and fumbles.
  • Pass: Potential for big gains, but higher risk of interceptions and incomplete passes.
  • Field Goal: Attempt to score 3 points if within range. Accuracy decreases with distance.
  • Razzle Dazzle: High-risk, high-reward play with unpredictable outcomes.

Penalties

Penalties occur randomly based on your play type or side. Common penalties include:

  • Offside: Defense penalty, 5 yards added to your yard line.
  • Holding: Offense penalty, 10 yards subtracted.
  • Pass Interference: Defense penalty, 15 yards added.
  • Illegal Formation: Offense penalty, 5 yards and loss of down.

Injuries

If you use the same play type repeatedly, a player might get injured! Injuries disable the play for the rest of the drive. Use varied strategies to avoid this.

Winning the Game

The game ends after the 4th quarter. Outscore the CPU to win. Good luck
